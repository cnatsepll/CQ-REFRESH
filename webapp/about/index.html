Hi, thanks for stopping by.



The quiz is a word association test.

Each question has a color type, for instance 'Patience' might be Green.

Depending on how you answer each question, points will be added to your overall score for its associated color.

At the end of the quiz, the overall scores for each color are compared together to come up with your result.

The charts then check those scores for each question answered and try to create a pattern out of the data, comparing word choice to final results.
